4. **Neutrino DOF counting bug caught and fixed**: Initial g_* calculation overcounted neutrino DOF (treating them as non-chiral with 2 spin x 2 particle/anti = 4 DOF per species instead of correct 1 helicity x 2 particle/anti = 2 DOF per species). Fixed before final script run.
