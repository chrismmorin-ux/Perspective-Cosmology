# AXM_0102 Axiom: Non-Triviality

**Tag**: 0102
**Type**: AXIOM
**Status**: CANONICAL
**Source**: core/01_universe.md

---

## Requires

- [DEF_0202: Points P]
- [DEF_0206: Content map C]

## Provides

- Non-trivial content distribution

---

## Statement

**U3 (Non-Triviality)**

```
∃ p, q ∈ P : C(p) ≠ C(q)
```

There exist at least two points with different content.

---

## Notes

This rules out the trivial universe where all points are identical.
It implies |P| ≥ 2 (see LEM_0400).
