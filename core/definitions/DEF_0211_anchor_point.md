# DEF_0211 Definition: Anchor Point

**Tag**: 0211
**Type**: DEFINITION
**Status**: CANONICAL
**Source**: core/02_perspective.md

---

## Requires

- [DEF_0202: Points P]
- [DEF_0210: Perspective π]

## Provides

- p: Anchor point of a perspective

---

## Statement

**p ∈ P** (Anchor Point)

The location of the perspective in U.

For a perspective π = (p, D, A), the anchor point p is where the perspective "is located."

---

## Notes

The anchor point is one of three components defining a perspective.
