## The Core Unresolved Question

All three critics converged on this:

> **"Were these formulas derived from first principles, or found by searching and then justified?"**

**Current answer**: We don't know. The sub-ppm matches are statistically unusual, but the methodology cannot distinguish genuine derivation from sophisticated pattern-matching.
