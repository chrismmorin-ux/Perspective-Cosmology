## The Theory

### Why This Works: Crystallization Dynamics

The framework proposes that physical constants arise through "crystallization" from a perfect algebraic structure (the "crystal") into the imperfect physical world (the "defect").

**The crystallization process**:

1. **The crystal basis**: The perfect structure has primes as orthogonal basis vectors, with division algebra dimensions {1, 2, 3, 4, 7, 8, 11} forming the fundamental directions

2. **Physical constants are projections**: Each constant is a projection from the crystal onto physical observables, filtered through the algebraic structures involved in that measurement

3. **Framework primes are resonances**: When two algebraic structures interact (e.g., EM and color), the crystallization "resonates" at their combined prime (e.g., 53 = 2² + 7²)

4. **Precision reflects purity**: The more purely a physical process involves specific algebraic structures, the more precisely its constants match the framework predictions

### The Selection Rule

**A framework prime p = a² + b² appears in physical constant X if and only if X measures an interaction involving both structure-a and structure-b.**

Examples:
- α_s measures EM-color coupling → involves 2 (EM) and 7 (color) → prime 53 = 2² + 7²
- Neutrino mixing measures generation-EM interaction → involves 2 (EM) and 3 (generations) → prime 13 = 2² + 3²
- Glueball mass is pure-color → involves 7 and 8 (both octonion) → prime 113 = 7² + 8²

### The Formula Structure

Physical constants follow the pattern:

```
Constant = (integer combination of dims) + (correction from cyclotomic Φ₆)
```

The main term uses dimensions directly. The correction term uses Φ₆(n) because:
- Φ₆(x) = x² - x + 1 relates squares (main structure) to linear (correction)
- Φ₆ appears at order 6 because 6 = 2 × 3 = dim(C) × Im_H connects EM and generations

## The Deeper Question

### Why Division Algebras?

The four division algebras (R, C, H, O) are mathematically unique: they are the ONLY normed division algebras over the reals (Hurwitz's theorem).

The framework suggests:
- **Physics is what it is because these are the only options**
- The dimensions {1, 2, 4, 8} and their imaginaries {0, 1, 3, 7} are not arbitrary choices
- Nature "had to" use these structures because no others exist with the required properties

### Why Primes?

Primes are **irreducible** — they cannot be factored. In the crystallization picture:
- Composite numbers can "split" during crystallization
- Primes cannot split — they are stable attractors
- Framework primes (sums of dimension squares) are doubly stable: prime AND algebraically structured

### Why Sum of Squares?

The sum-of-squares form a² + b² arises naturally from:
- **Norms**: |z|² = a² + b² for complex numbers z = a + bi
- **Pythagorean structure**: Distance in dimension space
- **Fermat's theorem**: Primes of form 4k+1 are exactly those expressible as a² + b²

The framework primes are exactly those where both a and b are division algebra dimensions.
