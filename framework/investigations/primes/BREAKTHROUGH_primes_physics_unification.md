## 1. The Core Discovery (Plain English)

### What Are Primes, Really?

**Primes are the dimensions of perfect separation.**

When things are completely independent — sharing absolutely nothing — they behave like prime numbers. The primes aren't arbitrary mathematical curiosities. They're the inevitable result of asking: "What are the basic building blocks when things are perfectly separate?"

### Why Do Primes Exist?

Because of how perspectives combine.

1. **The Crystal** = Everything that exists, with each part perfectly separate from every other part

2. **A Perspective** = A partial view — you can only see some of it at a time

3. **Combining Perspectives** = Seeing multiple parts together = **MULTIPLICATION**
   - Seeing dimension 2 alone → the number 2
   - Seeing dimension 3 alone → the number 3
   - Seeing dimensions 2 AND 3 together → 2 × 3 = 6

4. **Why Primes?** = Because anything else is redundant
   - "Dimension 4" would just be "dimension 2 accessed twice"
   - Only irreducible, independent dimensions can be fundamental
   - Those are exactly the primes

**Primes aren't chosen. They're forced by the requirement of non-redundancy.**
