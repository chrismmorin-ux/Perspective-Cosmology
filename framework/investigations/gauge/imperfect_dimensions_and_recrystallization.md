## Part IV: Connection to Standard Black Hole Physics

### 4.1 Bekenstein-Hawking Entropy: S = A/4

**Standard physics**: Black hole entropy is proportional to surface area, not volume. This is deeply mysterious — why should 3D information encode on a 2D surface?

**This frame**: The surface area IS the **dimensional footprint** — the record of imperfect dimensions before they were recrystallized.

```
Object (occupying semi-orthogonal dimensions)
    → falls toward black hole
    → dimensions get progressively merged
    → the "record" of original dimensional structure
        gets encoded on the horizon boundary
    → surface area = total dimensional content
```

**The holographic principle emerges naturally**: Information encodes on the boundary because that's where imperfect dimensions meet the recrystallization zone and get "stamped."

The entropy isn't stored on the surface — the surface **IS** the entropy, the record of dimensional structure that was there.

### 4.2 Accretion Discs: The Active Merger Zone

**Standard physics**: Matter spirals in, heated by friction and gravitational compression. Extremely energetic and chaotic.

**This frame**: The accretion disc is the **workshop** where gravity actively merges imperfect dimensions.

```
Accretion disc structure:

    Outer edge: mild recrystallization
        → dimensions slightly strained
        → moderate energy release

    Inner edge: intense recrystallization
        → dimensions actively merging
        → extreme energy release
        → chaotic, violent process
```

The heat and radiation are **byproducts of geometric simplification**. When two imperfect dimensions merge into one "more orthogonal" dimension, the energy difference must go somewhere.

### 4.3 Jets and Gamma Ray Bursts: The Energy Dividend

**Standard physics**: Somehow, energy escapes along polar axes in extremely collimated, high-energy jets. The mechanism is debated.

**This frame**: Jets are the **energy released when gravity successfully merges imperfect dimensions**.

```
Merger event (outside horizon):

    Two imperfect dimensions
        (each has "cost" to maintain)
            ↓
    Gravity merges them
            ↓
    One "more true" dimension remains
            ↓
    Energy difference = released
            ↓
    If outside horizon → ESCAPES as jet/burst
```

**Why collimated?** The merger happens along a specific axis — the "direction" of simplification. Energy escapes along that axis.

**Why so energetic?** Dimensional overhead is enormous. The "cost" of maintaining semi-orthogonality across cosmic scales is vast. Even small simplifications release huge energy.

**Prediction**: Jet energetics should show quantization related to which "types" of dimensions merged. Not continuous spectrum, but preferred energy levels.

### 4.4 Hawking Radiation: Boundary Fluctuations

**Standard physics**: Virtual particle pairs at horizon; one escapes, one falls in. Black hole slowly evaporates.

**This frame**: At the horizon boundary, imperfect dimensions occasionally **pinch off** before being fully merged.

```
At horizon:
    Imperfect dimensions being pulled in

    Occasionally:
        A small "piece" of semi-orthogonality
        fluctuates outward
        escapes before recrystallization completes

    = Hawking radiation
```

**Temperature** (inversely proportional to mass): Larger black holes recrystallize more "gently" — lower temperature. Smaller black holes recrystallize more "aggressively" — higher temperature, faster evaporation.

### 4.5 Information Paradox: Resolved

**The paradox**: If information falls into a black hole and the black hole evaporates, where does the information go? Quantum mechanics says it can't be destroyed.

**This frame**: Information is **never destroyed**. It becomes **orthogonal**.

```
Object with information
    → falls in
    → dimensions reoriented
    → information still exists
        but in dimensions orthogonal to outside observers

Outside observer:
    → sees information "disappear" past horizon
    → actually: information in dimensions they can't access

The object (from its perspective):
    → still has all its information
    → just can't communicate with outside
    → dimensions no longer overlap
```

The information is encoded on the horizon (Bekenstein-Hawking) because that's where the dimensional structure got "recorded" before reorientation.

When the black hole evaporates via Hawking radiation, the information re-emerges because the dimensional structure slowly "unwinds" back into accessible dimensions.

**No paradox**: Information is conserved, just in orthogonal dimensions during the black hole's lifetime.

### 4.6 Black Hole Evaporation: Recrystallization Completion

**Standard physics**: Black holes slowly evaporate via Hawking radiation. Smaller = faster evaporation.

**This frame**: As the black hole radiates, it's **completing its recrystallization work**.

```
Large black hole:
    Lots of imperfect dimensions to process
    Slow, gradual recrystallization
    Long lifetime

Small black hole:
    Less to process
    More aggressive recrystallization (higher temp)
    Shorter lifetime

Fully evaporated:
    Recrystallization COMPLETE for that region
    All imperfect dimensions merged/radiated away
    Left behind: flat, crystallized spacetime
        (or as close to crystal as possible)
```

**What remains after evaporation?** Not nothing — but maximally recrystallized spacetime. The "scar" of where a black hole was might be slightly more "orthogonal" than surrounding space.
