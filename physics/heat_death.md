## The Claim

Heat death is not "maximum disorder."

```
Heat death = state where:
- All unstable dimensions collapsed
- All stable dimensions hidden
- All perspectives reveal equivalent content
- Var(U) → 0
```

**Cyclic models**: Require violation of no-loop theorem.
